Forum De Beyrouth

Cultural experience at forum de beyrouth through food and local markets.


Join us in a tour of Forum De Beyrouth, where we will go through a tapestry of different experiences through food and culture. We interviewed many food stands in the forum and interviewed them to learn about their origins and their different varieties of dishes from Japanese mochi, to Mexican tacos, and many more. We also delve into stands that include innovative skincare products and other creative Lebanese artworks. This event has allowed us to experience a variety of cultures and the importance of food in those cultures, seeing the importance of the preservation of such cultures through food and art.
